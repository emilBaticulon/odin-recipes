Odin Recipes

Ingredients

  1. 2½ pounds pork neck bones
  2. 2 pounds pork spareribs
  3. water to cover
  4. 1 tablespoon salt
  5. 1½ onions, quartered
  6. 2 tomatoes, quartered, or more to taste
  7. 1 taro, peeled and cut into large chunks
  8. ½ cup fresh lemon juice
  9. 2 tablespoons fish sauce
  10. 1 pound bok choy, chopped
  11. 1 large leek, chopped
  12. 5 head-on shrimp
